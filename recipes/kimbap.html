Kimbap

Description

This is a recipe for korean kimbap. This is one of my favorite dishes. It is comprised of seaweed and various vegetables. It can also include meat such as tuna, beef, or pork.

I hope my instructions are clear and that you can enjoy this recipe as much as I enjoy making it. Good luck to you!

Recipe

  • Seaweed
  • Rice
  • Carrot
  • Meat (optional)
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the rice and egg
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Cut the carrot
  4. Place the seaweed flat down. Add rice on top
  5. Add vegetables and meat on top
  6. Roll tightly
  7. Cut and eat while it's warm!
