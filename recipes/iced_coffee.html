Iced Coffee

Description

This is my recipe for iced coffee.

My favorite coffee to drink is iced coffee. I really love it and I hope you like my recipe!

Recipe

  • Coffee beans or pod
  • Coffee grinder or coffee maker
  • Cup
  • Ice
  • Milk
  • Sugar or vanilla powder (vanilla latte)

Steps

  1. Grind the coffee beans or roast the pod
  2. Put the ice in the cup to the top
  3. Pour the hot coffee into the cup
  4. Add milk and optional sugar and powder
  5. Mix everything together
  6. Enjoy your coffee!
