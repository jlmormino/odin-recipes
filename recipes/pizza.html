Pizza

Description

This is my recipe for Pizza. There are many ways to make pizza but I like pizza that is more simple, and a more traditional italian way.

Today I will be explaining how to make a pizza from scratch, with the use of a just a few key ingredients. I hope you enjoy my recipe as much as I enjoy making it!

Recipe

  • Flour
  • Red Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Spread out of the dough flat
  2. Add the tomato sauce
  3. Cover the top with cheese
  4. Add mozzarella cheese on top
  5. Cook in oven for 10 minutes
  6. Take out and enjoy!
